A social currency
turned into a crypto-currency!

From Bitcoin to Dogecoin, a variety of cryptocurrencies have spawned into existence since the inception of Bitcoin in 2009. Today, nearly a decade later, we have entire countries adopting cryptocurrencies as their official, state-sponsored, fiat currency, redefining how we store and transact our money.
When considering money, however, security is a natural concern and it is undeniable that while blockchain technology has flourished, other fields of computing technology have progressed significantly as well. In a world where transactions are stored and verified on the blockchain, quantum computing, in particular, brings serious security conerns to the table. In an effort to future proof cryptocurrencies, we've created Brownie Points; a revolutionary cryptocurrency whose transactions are signed with quantum resistant algorithms that thwart any possible security concerns that may emerge in the near future.
